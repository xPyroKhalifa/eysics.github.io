Solution Swift FF

The SOLUTION SWIFT™ FF shoe is designed for all-court players covering the net and baseline. It features a flexible sole design that’s functional for multi-directional movements. This shoe’s midsole is formed with FLYTEFOAM® cushioning. It’s a lightweight and durable foam that increases shock absorption. Meanwhile, the midfoot’s TWISTRUSS™ support unit helps you experience quick and confident cuts on court. The shoe’s outsole mirrors the SOLUTION SPEED™ FF 2 shoe’s sole design. ASICS’ Underlines Commitment to Move Every Mind  [April 3, 2023 – Hoofddorp, NL] – ASICS today launches a global campaign highlighting the power of exercise to help women cope with everyday stresses. The campaign marks the launch of ASICS’ new NAGINO™ Collection and highlights the positive influence that movement can play on our mental state. The campaign was developed in response to ASICS 2022 State of Mind Index, which revealed that globally, women tend to exercise less than men and, as a result, experience a lower state of mind.
